About our Company

Mama Nature is an organic plant-based restaurant and store offering high quality delicious foods and drinks made with whole, unprocessed, gluten-free and sugar-free ingredients. We aim to be as local, seasonal, and eco-friendly as possible.

Mama Nature’s mission is to offer and remind people about real food and our connection to Mother Nature, gather a conscious community around health and well-being, and inspire people to make better choices for themselves and for the environment.

Working at Mama Nature is not for everyone. It requires individuals who have impeccable integrity, truthfulness, healthy life values, a growth mindset, a service mindset, who are solution-focused, and who have a great ability to go through challenges and issues in a positive manner.

Our Story

In the heart of Bar, Montenegro, Mama Nature brings healing and nourishing plant-based cuisine to life. We celebrate the bounty of nature with locally sourced organic ingredients and conscious cooking.

Our Chef Myriam Bakhti, also known as Raw Mama Nature, have for many years taught online workshops on gourmet raw plant-based cuisine, and authored books like "Easy Recipies for Beginners", "The Raw Vegan Bread Book" and "Sweet Life".

Myriam decided that it was time to open a restaurant where she could share her passion for healthy food with the world.

If you already enjoy plant-based cuisine or are curious to try, we would love to cook for you.

At Mama Nature, we offer a variety of services to enhance your dining experience. From private dining events to catering services, we are here to make your special occasions memorable. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our guests.
